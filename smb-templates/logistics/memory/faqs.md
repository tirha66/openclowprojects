## Tracking & Status

**Q: Where is my delivery / parcel?**
A: Your parcel is currently [status]. It's expected to be delivered [today between X–Y / tomorrow / on DATE]. You can also track it live here: [tracking URL]

**Q: My tracking hasn't updated in a while — is something wrong?**
A: Tracking can sometimes lag by a few hours, especially in transit between depots. If it hasn't updated in more than 24 hours, I'll look into it for you right now. Can you share your order/tracking number?

**Q: What does "[tracking status]" mean?**
A: "[Out for delivery]" means your driver has your parcel and is on their route today. "[In transit]" means it's moving between our facilities. "[Awaiting collection]" means it's ready at [depot/locker location] for you to pick up.

**Q: Can I get a more specific delivery time?**
A: Our drivers work routes, so we can give you a 2-hour window closer to the time. You'll get an SMS/notification when the driver is [X] stops away. Would you like to be added to live notifications?

## Delivery Issues

**Q: My delivery was marked as delivered but I didn't receive it.**
A: I'm sorry about that — let me look into this right away. Could you check: around your property (door, side gate, with a neighbour), and your phone for any delivery notification photos? I'll also pull the driver's delivery confirmation. Tracking number?

**Q: My parcel arrived damaged.**
A: I'm really sorry to hear that. Please take photos of the damage and the packaging before opening further. I'll log a damage claim for you now — we'll arrange a replacement/refund. Can you share your order number and a few photos?

**Q: A delivery attempt was made but I wasn't home. What happens now?**
A: The driver will have left a card or notification. You can: (1) Reschedule for another day, (2) Collect from [depot address], or (3) Nominate a safe place or neighbour. Which would you prefer? I can sort it right now.

**Q: My parcel was supposed to arrive today but it hasn't.**
A: I apologise for the delay. Let me check on this — sometimes delays happen due to [traffic/weather/high volume]. I'll find out where it is and give you an updated ETA. Tracking number please?

**Q: The driver left my parcel somewhere unsafe / with the wrong person.**
A: That shouldn't happen — I'm sorry. Please share your address and order number and I'll raise this with dispatch immediately. We take safe delivery very seriously.

## Scheduling & Rescheduling

**Q: Can I change my delivery date or time?**
A: Yes, as long as the parcel hasn't been dispatched yet. Once it's with a driver, we have limited ability to redirect. What date works better for you?

**Q: Can I request a specific delivery time window?**
A: We offer [morning / afternoon / evening] slots where available. Premium timed delivery is [available for £X / included in your plan]. Would you like to book a slot?

**Q: Can I have my delivery left in a safe place?**
A: Absolutely — just tell me where (e.g. "behind the gate", "in the porch") and I'll add it to your delivery instructions. Note that we can't be responsible for parcels left unattended.

**Q: Can I redirect my delivery to a different address?**
A: If the parcel hasn't been dispatched, yes. Once it's en route, redirection may incur a [£X] fee and a 1-day delay. What's the new address?
