## Booking Policy

- Appointments can be booked online at **www.glowstudio.co.uk/book**, by calling **020 7946 0832**, or by messaging us here.
- A **credit or debit card** is required to secure all bookings. You are not charged until the appointment.
- New clients booking colour services of 90 minutes or more are required to complete a **patch test at least 48 hours before** their appointment. This is non-negotiable for safety reasons.
- We recommend arriving **5 minutes early** for your appointment.

---

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancellations or rescheduling made **48+ hours in advance:** No charge.
- Cancellations made **24–48 hours in advance:** 50% of the service cost will be charged.
- Cancellations made **less than 24 hours in advance** or **no-shows:** 100% of the service cost will be charged.

We understand life happens — if you need to cancel, please let us know as early as possible and we'll always do our best to accommodate you.
