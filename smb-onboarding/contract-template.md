## 2. Scope of Services

The Provider agrees to set up and manage an AI-powered employee ("AI Employee") on behalf of the Client. This includes:

### 2.1 Initial Setup (One-Time)
- Intake and discovery session to understand the Client's business
- Configuration of the AI Employee's personality, tone, and knowledge base
- Integration with agreed messaging channels (e.g. Telegram, WhatsApp, webchat)
- Loading of business information, FAQs, services, and pricing
- Setting up escalation rules and after-hours responses
- Testing across all channels
- Handover walkthrough (recorded video)

### 2.2 Ongoing Monthly Management
- Monitoring of AI Employee performance
- Updates to knowledge base as the Client's business changes
- Monthly performance report
- One scheduled check-in call per month
- Minor adjustments to tone, escalation rules, or FAQs (up to [X] changes per month)

## 5. Intellectual Property

- Any content, workflows, or configurations created by the Provider using the Client's business information remain the **intellectual property of the Client** once fully paid for.
- Templates, frameworks, and methodologies developed by the Provider remain the **intellectual property of the Provider**.
- The Client grants the Provider permission to use their business information solely for the purpose of configuring and operating the AI Employee.

---

## 6. Confidentiality and Data Handling

- Both Parties agree to keep each other's confidential business information private and not share it with third parties without consent.
- The Provider will handle all Client data with care and will not use it for any purpose beyond delivering the agreed services.
- The Client is responsible for ensuring they have the right to share any customer data or content provided to the Provider.
- The Provider will comply with applicable data protection laws. The Client acknowledges that messages handled by the AI Employee may be processed by third-party AI infrastructure (e.g. AI model providers).

---

## 7. AI Disclosure

The Client acknowledges and agrees that:

- Communications on their behalf will be handled, in part or in full, by an artificial intelligence system.
- The AI Employee is not a human and may make errors or misunderstand requests.
- The Client is responsible for setting appropriate escalation rules to ensure human oversight where needed.
- The Client should inform their customers that an AI may be handling communications, where required by law or platform policy.

---

## 8. Cancellation and Refunds

### 8.1 Cancellation by the Client
The Client may cancel this agreement by providing **30 days' written notice** (email is acceptable). The Client will be charged for the final month's retainer in full.

### 8.2 Cancellation by the Provider
The Provider may terminate this agreement with **30 days' written notice**, or immediately in cases of non-payment, abusive behaviour, or if the Client is using the AI Employee for unlawful purposes.

### 8.3 Refunds
- The setup fee is **non-refundable** once work has commenced.
- Monthly retainers are **non-refundable** for the current billing period.
- If the Provider cancels without cause, a pro-rated refund of the current month's retainer will be issued.

---

## 9. Limitation of Liability

- The Provider's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the three months preceding the claim.
- The Provider is not liable for any indirect, consequential, or reputational damages arising from the use of the AI Employee.
- The AI Employee is a tool to assist the Client's business — the Client remains responsible for their customer relationships and business decisions.
